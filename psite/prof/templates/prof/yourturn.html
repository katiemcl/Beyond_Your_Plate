interview guide

This Notion page holds our interview guide. Some of the questions are more specific to Sakura, so please feel free to make a duplicate and adjust them to your local restaurants and any specific questions you might have.

üí° Helpful Tips

  • Recording: ask for permission to voice record your interview so that you can be fully present in the conversation
  • Compensation: Before ending the interview, ask if there is anything you can do in return for them giving you their time. This is time that they could have spent working or relaxing, but instead they spent it with you!
